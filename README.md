# NFL Offensive Play Styles

## About

This past NFL season, it occured to me - I've never *really* considered how NFL teams play. I mean I have, but not with any more thought than idley listening to a podcast. This team plays under center a lot, this team out of the gun, Andy Reid loves his toy receivers and they dink and dunk...

But, like, how do the Saints play? When the Colts are playing the Panthers on a random Sunday, I have no idea what to expect.

That's the point of this exploration - How do NFL offenses play? What distinguishes one team from another? What are the significant traits or characteristics of NFL play styles? Are there major groupings of teams or styles? What are other teams like my team or my team's opponent this weekend? And, if we get crazy... are some styles better than others?

I've approached this with two techniques:

### Principle Component Analysis
- What are the significant traits or characteristics of NFL play styles?
- What separates teams most in terms of style?

### Clustering
- Are there major groupings of teams or styles?
- What are other teams like my team or my team's opponent this weekend?
- [cautiously] ... are some styles better than others?
